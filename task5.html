DARK


Description

A missing child sets four families on a frantic hunt for answers as they unearth a mind-bending mystery that spans three generations. Watch all you want. This suspenseful series from Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese won a Grimme-Preis award for television fiction


Season 1

Description

The story follows characters from the fictional town of Winden, Germany, as they pursue the truth in the aftermath of a child's disappearance. They follow connections between four estranged families to unravel a sinister time travel conspiracy that spans several generations.


Season 2

Description

The second season continues the intertwining families' attempts to reunite with their missing loved ones, several months after the first-season finale, in 2020, 1987 and 1954, respectively. Additional story-lines are set in 2053 and 1921.


Season 3

Description

When two children go missing in a small German town, its sinful past is exposed along with the double lives and fractured relationships that exist among four families as they search for the kids.
